Privacy Policy

Knot App

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

Knot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Knot mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number — used to create and identify your account
  • Display name — optional, used to identify you to others in your debt network
  • Debt information — amounts, currencies, notes, and counterparty phone numbers you enter when declaring debts

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device push notification tokens — used to send you notifications about debt activity
  • Usage data — basic app interaction data to improve the service

1.3 Information From Your Device

  • Contacts — with your permission, we access your device contacts solely to identify which of your contacts are already on Knot. We do not store your full contact list on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Knot account
  • Enable debt declaration, confirmation, and dispute between users
  • Detect debt cycles in your network and present resolution opportunities
  • Send push notifications about debt activity (declarations, confirmations, disputes, and cycle detections)
  • Send SMS notifications to people you declare debts against who are not yet on Knot
  • Improve and maintain the App

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other Knot users — your display name and phone number are visible to users you share debts or cycles with. No information is visible to users outside your debt network.
  • With service providers — we use Supabase (database and authentication), Expo (push notifications), and Twilio (SMS). These providers process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies.
  • For legal compliance — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Visibility and Privacy

Knot is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • You can only see debts you are directly party to (as creditor or debtor)
  • You can only see debt cycles you are a participant in
  • No debt information is visible to users outside your network
  • We do not display advertising or share data with advertisers

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Settled debts are archived but not permanently deleted, as they may be needed for dispute resolution history. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the address below.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), row-level security on our database ensuring users can only access their own data, and secure authentication via phone number verification. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Knot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and backend: supabase.com/privacy
  • Expo / EAS — push notifications and app distribution: expo.dev/privacy
  • Twilio — SMS delivery: twilio.com/legal/privacy

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the App or by SMS. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The effective date at the top of this document will always reflect the most recent revision.
